# Privacy Policy for BloodDonor

**Effective Date: May 23, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
BloodDonor ("the App") is developed by **Wai Min Kyaw** as a community service tool to manage blood donor registration and donation history. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of the donors' sensitive medical and personal information.

## 2. Data Collection
The App collects and stores the following information locally on your device:
*   **Personal Information**: Name, Date of Birth, Age, Gender, Weight, and Address.
*   **Health Information**: Blood Group, Blood Pressure readings, Hemoglobin (Hb%) levels, and Infection Screening results (HIVAb, HBsAg, HCVAb, and Malaria ICT).
*   **Contact Information**: Phone Number, Messenger, Viber, and Telegram usernames.

## 3. Purpose of Data Use
The information collected is used strictly for:
*   Verifying donor eligibility based on medical criteria.
*   Maintaining a historical record of donations (milestones).
*   Allowing medical staff to contact donors for urgent needs.
*   Generating unique Donor IDs for identification.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
*   **Local Storage**: All data is stored locally on the device's internal storage using a Room (SQLite) database. 
*   **No Cloud Sync**: We do not upload your data to any external servers or cloud services.
*   **Obfuscation**: The application code is obfuscated to prevent unauthorized reverse engineering or data theft.

## 5. Data Sharing
*   **WiFi Sync**: Data is only shared between devices when the user manually initiates the "Sync Data" feature over a local WiFi network. This is a direct peer-to-peer transfer.
*   **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell, trade, or share donor information with any third-party advertisers or data brokers.

## 6. User Control and Deletion
Users have full control over the data.
*   **Data Deletion**: All donor records and history are permanently deleted from the device if the App is uninstalled or if the "Clear Data" option is selected in Android system settings.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:
contact me at waiminkyaw@gmail.com
